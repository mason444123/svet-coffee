Quiet Mornings.

Свет (Light) was born from the desire to create a sanctuary. A place where the ritual of coffee is respected, unhurried, and precise. We believe in high-end minimalism, focusing only on what matters: the bean, the water, and the quiet moment before the day begins.

Sourced from single estates, roasted light to preserve origin character, and brewed with intention. Our spaces are designed to reflect the clarity we seek in our cups—luminous, calm, and grounded.
